Some of My Favourite Songs

Background: Music for me is one of my greatest passions, it has influenced how I view the world to an extent, music is a powerful work of art, I will be picking 5 Random Songs from my playlist, and explaining why I like them, and some of my favourite parts about that song

1. Head High - Joey Bada$$s

Click on the cover art to hear this song!


Why this song is special to me

  • The Message: This track finds Joey reflecting on the meaning of life, after hes lost several people close to him, mentioning the late Capital STEEZ and XXXTENTACION
  • Production: This song has a incredible beat produced by Statik Selektah, incorperating a soulful jazz sound into the song
  • Lyrical Content: Despite the topics of grief and loss, the song encourages perseverance and keeping your head up during tough times

2. Sacrifices - J. Cole (feat. EARTHGANG, Smino & Saba)

Click on the cover art to hear this song!


Why this song is special to me

  • Vulnerability: On this song, J. Cole opens upabout his relationship, fatherhood, and personal growth
  • Lyrical Content:J. Cole's verse shows a vivid imagination and reflection on his personal growth
  • Features: This song features EarthGang, Smino, and Saba, each delivering passionate verses

3. Saint Pablo - Kanye West

Click on the cover art to hear this song!


Why this song is special to me

  • Self-Reflection: Kanye opens up about his struggles with fame, debt, mental health, and public perception.
  • Lyrical Content: The writing balances braggadocio with humility, offering lines like "I know I'm the most influential / That time cover was just confirmation." showing sharp, thoughtful, and self-aware lyrics
  • Sampha: British singer and songwriter Sampha is featured on this song, giving us a passionate and melodic hook that enhances the song to the fullest

4. 1Train - A$AP Rocky (feat. Kendrick Lamar, Joey Bada$$, Yelawolf, Danny Brown, Action Bronson & Big K.R.I.T.)

Click on the cover art to hear this song!


Why this song is special to me

  • Stacked Feature List: The song features a stacked roster of top-tier rappers such as Kendrick Lamar, Joey Bada$$, Yelawolf, Danny Brown, Action Bronson, and Big K.R.I.T.—each delivering unique and high-energy verses.
  • Production: The grimy boom bap production by Hit-Boy elevates the New York Cypher energy
  • Lyrical Content: Rocky strips away the tradition of including a hook, as this track is just straight rapping

5. Ronald Reagen Era - Kendrick Lamar

Click on the cover art to hear this song!


Why this song is special to me

  • Powerful Political Commentary:Kendrick uses the Reagan era as a backdrop to critique the long-term effects of 1980s policies on Black communities, touching on systemic racism, drug epidemics, and generational trauma.
  • Production: The beat, produced by Tae Beast, is dark and moody, matching the song’s tense subject matter.
  • Lyrical Content: Kendrick’s verses are filled with vivid imagery, clever wordplay, and urgent delivery.
